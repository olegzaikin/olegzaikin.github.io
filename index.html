Oleg Zaikin

I am a leading researcher at Matrosov Institute for System Dynamics and Control Theory (ISDCT SB RAS), a research institute of the Russian Academy of Sciences.

Previously, I was a research assistant at the Computer Science Department of Swansea University, United Kingdom.

I did my Ph.D. (candidate of technical sciences in Russia) under the supervision of Alexander A. Semenov at ISDCT SB RAS. My thesis was about applying parallel SAT solving to cryptanalysis of stream ciphers.

Currently, my research is mainly focused on solving hard cryptanalysis and combinatorial problems via Cube-and-Conquer.
